School of the Quadrivium Programs

Associate of Arts

Whether you're working towards a 4-year degree in humanities, social sciences, or business, or looking to complete training at a professional school, our Associate of Arts degree will prepare you for success. The School of the Quadrivium is dedicated to the basic skills necessary for every person to succeed in our modern world: Literacy, Mathematics and Scientific Reasoning.

The Associate of Arts degree is awarded to students who have successfully met the following requirements:

  1. Successful completion of 61 credit hours, including the following General Education Requirements:
    • GRA-100: First Year Writing
    • LOG-100: First Year Scientific Reasoning
    • RHE-100: First Year Rhetoric
    • MUS-100: Music Appreciation or SPA-100: Art Appreciation
    • One Literature course
    • One Mathematics course
    • One Natural Science course
    • One Social or Behavioral Science course
  2. A grade point average on credit hours attempted of no less than 2.0.
  3. At least 16 credits hours in residence at Romanorum Theological University.
